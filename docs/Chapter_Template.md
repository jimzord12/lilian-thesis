### **Why This Matters**

- **Goal:** The "Elevator Pitch."
- **Content:** Why is this specific technology/concept critical _right now_? What happens if we fail to implement it?
- **Length:** 100–150 words.

### **Literature Review and Background**

- **Goal:** The Academic Foundation.
- **Content:** How has this technology evolved? From [Old Method] to [New Method]. Cite key historical and recent studies.
- **Length:** 200–250 words.

### **Technical Challenges and Issues**

- **Goal:** The Constraints.
- **Content:** What makes this hard in a _maritime_ environment? (e.g., bandwidth, corrosion, intermittent power, regulations).
- **Length:** 200–250 words.

### **Current Technologies and Approaches**

- **Goal:** The State-of-the-Art (SOTA).
- **Content:** What specific algorithms, protocols, or hardware are used _today_? Be technical and specific (e.g., specific standards, model names).
- **Length:** 250–300 words.

### **Applications and Use Cases**

- **Goal:** Real-World Proof.
- **Content:** Who is using this? (e.g., Maersk, Navy, Smart Ports). What are the tangible benefits observed?
- **Length:** 150–200 words.

### **Future Directions and Research Gaps**

- **Goal:** The Horizon.
- **Content:** What is missing? What will the next generation (e.g., 6G, AI) bring? This sets up the "Research Gaps" for the conclusion.
- **Length:** 150–200 words.
