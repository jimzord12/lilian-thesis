## English Version

| Performance Metric | Baseline (Traditional Methods) | AI/ML Impact (Predictive Analytics) | Improvement (%) |
|:-------------------|:-------------------------------|:------------------------------------|:----------------|
| Transit Time Error | 25% - 35%                      | 6.8% (MAPE)                         | ~75% - 80%      |
| On-Time Delivery   | 76%                            | 92%+                                | +21%            |
| Expedited Costs    | Standard                       | Reduced by 17%                      | 17%             |
| Delivery Variance  | Standard                       | Reduced by 75%                      | 75%             |
